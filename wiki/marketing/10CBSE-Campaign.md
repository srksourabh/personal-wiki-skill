# 10CBSE Social Media Campaign (15-Day Autonomous)

## Overview

Fully autonomous 15-day social media campaign for **10CBSE**, a board exam preparation SaaS. Campaign consists of **60 posts** across **4 platforms** (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X) with daily themes and rotating messaging.

**Status:** ✅ Ready for autonomous deployment via Postiz API

---

## Campaign Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Product** | 10CBSE (CBSE board exam prep) |
| **Website** | www.10cbse.com |
| **Posts** | 60 posts (15 days × 4 platforms) |
| **Duration** | 15 days (repeating monthly) |
| **Platforms** | LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X |
| **Daily Posting Times** | 9:30 AM, 11:30 AM, 2:30 PM, 4:30 PM IST |
| **Target Audience** | Parents, CBSE Class 10 students |
| **ICP** | Parents willing to invest ₹2,000-5,000/subject on board prep |
| **Format** | Autonomous scheduling via Postiz API — no copy-paste needed |

## Target Audience Profile

### Primary (Parents - B2B)
- Parents of Class 10 CBSE students
- Willingness to invest ₹2,000-5,000 per subject on board coaching
- Pain point: generic coaching covers unnecessary syllabus bloat
- Desire: daughters/sons to study less, score better through focused prep

### Secondary (Students - B2C)
- CBSE Class 10 students (14-16 years old)
- Digital natives, prefer app-based learning
- Time-constrained (want to score well without excessive study hours)
- Influenced by peer recommendations and social proof

### Messaging Angles (per 15-day cycle)

1. **Exam Relevance** — "Study what actually gets asked"
   - 10 years CBSE past papers analyzed
   - 134 chapters distilled to essentials
   - No fluff

2. **Social Proof** — Trust via testimonials
   - Real parent testimonials
   - Student success stories
   - Quantified results (30% less study, 30% better scores)

3. **Differentiation** — Why 10CBSE is unique
   - Data-driven approach
   - Past paper analysis
   - Targeted preparation vs generic coaching

4. **Urgency & CTA** — Limited-time offers
   - 14-day free trial
   - "Exams are coming"
   - Sign-ups via 10cbse.com

5. **Feature Highlights** — Deep-dive on key features
   - Smart practice tests
   - Performance analytics
   - Subject-specific learning paths

6. **Community & Belonging** — Join thousands of students
   - Parent community testimonials
   - Student peer network
   - Success network effect

7. **Affordability** — Price positioning
   - Better than generic coaching
   - Flexible payment
   - Results guarantee

8. **Urgency (Exam Calendar)** — Season-specific messaging
   - "Exams in 30 days"
   - "Score high with focused prep"
   - Time-sensitive CTAs

## Platform-Specific Messaging

### LinkedIn (B2B Parents, Education Professionals)
- **Tone:** Professional, data-driven, ROI-focused
- **Format:** Longer-form posts (150-300 chars), professional language
- **CTA:** "Learn more," "Join 10,000+ parents," "Schedule a demo"
- **Hashtags:** #CBSE #Education #BoardExams #StudentSuccess

### Instagram (Students, Visual Appeal)
- **Tone:** Relatable, casual, visual-first
- **Format:** Visual + caption (50-150 chars), emoji-heavy, story-friendly
- **CTA:** "Link in bio," "DM for details," "Try free"
- **Hashtags:** #CBSE #BoardExams #StudentLife #StudyTips

### Facebook (Community, Parents, Testimonials)
- **Tone:** Conversational, community-oriented, trusted
- **Format:** Medium-form (200-400 chars), conversation-starters, testimonials
- **CTA:** "Sign up," "Learn more," "Join our community"
- **Hashtags:** #CBSE #Education #BoardPrepByParents

### X/Twitter (Direct Pitch, Quick Tips)
- **Tone:** Witty, direct, pithy
- **Format:** Ultra-short (100-140 chars), punchy takeaways, viral potential
- **CTA:** "Link," "Learn more," "Try free"
- **Hashtags:** #CBSE #BoardExams #Education
