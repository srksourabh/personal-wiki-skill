# UDS Social Media Campaign (15-Day Autonomous)

## Overview

Fully autonomous 15-day social media campaign for **Ultimate Digital Solutions (UDS)**, a Howrah-based product-focused digital studio. Campaign consists of **59 posts** across **4 platforms** (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X) with daily themes emphasizing product thinking, technical capability, and founder-friendly positioning.

**Status:** ✅ Ready for autonomous deployment via Postiz API
