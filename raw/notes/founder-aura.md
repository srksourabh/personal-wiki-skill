# Founder Aura (Senra Notes)

**Source:** David Senra, Founders Podcast.
**Example:** Meng To (Design+Code), Steve Jobs, Rick Rubin.

### What is it?
Aura is the result of **High Taste + High Technical Skill**. It is the reason people buy an Apple product or a Meng To course without looking at the features list. They are buying the founder's judgment.

### The "Aura" Checklist:
- Does it feel "inevitable"?
- Does it reflect a single, coherent vision?
- Does it ignore "market research" in favor of "founder instinct"?

### Meng To's Specific Aura:
- **Glassmorphism & Depth:** His signature visual style.
- **Educational Authority:** He doesn't just teach tools; he teaches "The Way of the Designer-Coder."
